# Context-Aware Chatbot - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Click a Product
Go to the **Products** page and click on any product card (e.g., PRUMillion Protect, PRUWealth, etc.)

### Step 2: Open Chatbot
Click the **robot icon** 🤖 in the bottom-right corner to open the eHeart Advisor chatbot

### Step 3: Ask Questions
Ask any question about the product you're viewing:
- "What are the benefits?"
- "How much does it cost?"
- "What age is eligible?"
- "How does it work?"
- "What are the returns?"

**The chatbot will answer specifically about the product you're viewing!** ✨

## 🎓 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Read the PDF First
The chatbot complements the PDF, it doesn't replace it. Read the PDF for complete details, then ask the chatbot for clarifications.

### Tip 2: Ask Specific Questions
Instead of "Tell me everything," ask specific questions like:
- "What are the benefits?"
- "How much does it cost?"
- "What's the age range?"

### Tip 3: Use Natural Language
You don't need to use formal language. Ask naturally:
- ✅ "What are the benefits?"
- ✅ "How much is this?"
- ✅ "Can my 50-year-old client get this?"

### Tip 4: Check the Product Card
The chatbot shows the product card in its response. Click it to view the full PDF again if needed.

---

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Chatbot doesn't know which product I'm viewing
**Solution:** Make sure you clicked the product card first. The PDF viewer should be showing the product.

### Problem: Chatbot gives generic answer
**Solution:** Try asking a more specific question like "What are the benefits?" instead of just "Tell me."

### Problem: Chatbot shows wrong product
**Solution:** Click the correct product card again and wait for the PDF to load.
