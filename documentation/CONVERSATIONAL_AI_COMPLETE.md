## 💬 Example Conversations

### **Example 1: Asking About Benefits**

**You:** "What are the benefits of VUL?"

**The Big Benefits:**
• **Dual Purpose** - You're not just buying insurance, you're also investing. Two birds, one stone!

Want to see which VUL products we have available? I can show you options that fit different budgets."

---

### **Example 2: Asking About Coverage**

**You:** "What's covered?"

**Bot:** "Good question! Let me walk you through what our products typically cover:

**Standard Coverage (Most Products):**
• **Death Benefit** - The main protection. Your client's beneficiaries receive a lump sum if something happens.
• **Total & Permanent Disability (TPD)** - If your client can't work due to disability, they're covered.
• **Critical Illness** - Major illnesses like cancer, heart attack, stroke - we've got them covered.
• **Accidental Death Benefit** - Extra coverage if death is due to an accident.

**Plus, depending on the product:**
• Hospital income benefits
• Waiver of premium (if disabled, premiums are waived)
• Accelerated benefits for terminal illness

Each product has its own specific coverage details in the Product Primer. Want me to show you a specific product so you can see the exact coverage?"

---

### **Example 3: Comparing Products**

**You:** "Compare VUL vs Traditional"

**Bot:** "Great question! Let me break down the key differences for you:

**VUL (Variable Unit-Linked Insurance)** 📊
Think of this as insurance meets investment. Your premiums are split between life coverage and investment funds. The investment part can grow based on market performance, giving you potential for higher returns. It's perfect for clients who want their money to work harder and are comfortable with some market risk.

**Traditional Life Insurance** 🛡️
This is your classic, reliable life insurance. You get guaranteed protection and guaranteed cash value growth - no surprises. The returns are more modest but predictable. Great for clients who value stability and peace of mind over high growth potential.

**My recommendation?**
• If your client is younger and wants wealth building → Go with VUL
• If they prefer guaranteed returns and stability → Choose Traditional

Would you like me to show you specific products in either category?"

---

### **Example 4: Asking About Premiums**

**You:** "How much does it cost?"

**Bot:** "Ah, the budget question - always important! Here's the deal:

**Premium Flexibility:**
Our products start from as low as ₱1,000/month, going up depending on coverage and age. The beauty is we have options for different budgets!

**Payment Options:**
• **Regular Pay** - Pay throughout the policy term (most affordable monthly)
• **Limited Pay** - Pay for 5, 10, or 15 years, then you're done (higher monthly but finite)
• **Single Pay** - One payment and you're covered for life (highest upfront but no more payments)

**What affects the premium?**
• Client's age (younger = lower premium)
• Coverage amount (more coverage = higher premium)
• Product type (VUL vs Traditional)
• Payment term (longer term = lower monthly)

Tell me your client's age and budget, and I'll show you exactly what they can get!"
